About Me

An Aspiring Professional Web Developer

My desire to learn how to develop began around 2014 on a blogging platform called Tumblr. It all started when I wanted to customize the layout of the current theme I had applied to my blog. Then I wanted to add other features, like a music player at the bottom of the screen, a sidebar that remains on the left and right side even when the user would scroll down. The possibilities were endless! After going down a different path, I finally made the decision to formally learn how to develop by enrolling into the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp.
